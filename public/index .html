About us

SPECIALIZED IN SPICY MODERN

GroveGrain a unit of SHIVSHIDH BUSINESS GROUP. Flour Mill founded in the year 2020 by the founder Mr. Shivaji Kolekar . Being in the Atta business, we deeply understand the profound connection Indians have with their food. Our commitment is to provide healthy, nutritious, and fresh Atta to our customers. Many of our customers have been with us since the inception. Over time, we have consistently upgraded our Flour Mill machinery with the latest technology to meet the high-quality expectations of our consumers. We use Red Sandstone, which helps maintain a lower temperature of the flour during grinding, preserving the taste and nutrients in our flours. We deliver a wide variety of hot and fresh Atta to locations across India, including major cities like Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Kolkata, Chennai, Hyderabad, Pune, Ahmedabad, Jaipur, and more. Our product range includes a diverse selection of flours (Atta) to cater to the unique preferences of our customers. Additionally, we offer customized Atta to meet your special dietary needs.

We are committed to provide healthy products to the nation with love & care and no compromise in service and quality at the minimum cost possible. Brown Chakki is a complete package of health benefits loaded with essential nutrients.
